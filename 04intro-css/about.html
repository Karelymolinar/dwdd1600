About Us

Who We Are

We are a museum dedicated to the belief that art is not a luxury — it is a necessity. Founded by a community of historians, educators, and art lovers, our mission is to make five centuries of human creativity accessible to everyone: the student encountering a masterwork for the first time, the scholar deepening a lifetime of study, and the curious visitor who simply wandered in. Our galleries are designed not as a shrine to the past, but as a space for conversation. One where history speaks directly to the present. We are curators, yes, but more than that we are storytellers. Every label, every gallery, every carefully chosen work is our invitation to you:

  • Look closely
  • Ask questions
  • Leave knowing something about the world and yourself

Our Goal

Our goal is simple, though the work is never finished: to bring the full arc of Western art history to life in a way that is honest, inclusive, and endlessly engaging. We strive to preserve and present works spanning five centuries not merely as artifacts of the past, but as active participants in how we understand culture, identity, and human progress today. We are committed to building a space where rigorous scholarship and genuine accessibility coexist. Where an expert and a first-time visitor can stand before the same canvas and both walk away enriched. Beyond our walls, we seek to extend that experience through education programs, community partnerships, and digital resources that ensure art history belongs to everyone, not just those who can walk through our doors.

Our Values

Accessibility
Great art and the knowledge surrounding it should be available to all, regardless of background, education, or means.
Integrity
We present history honestly, including its complexities, contradictions, and uncomfortable truths.
Curiosity
We believe questions matter as much as answers, and we foster an environment where looking closely and thinking deeply are always encouraged.
Preservation
We are stewards of cultural heritage, holding these works in trust for future generations.
Community
This museum exists because of and for the people it serves. We are not an institution apart from our community — we are an expression of it.
Education
Learning does not end at the gallery door. We are committed to programs and resources that extend the conversation beyond the museum.
