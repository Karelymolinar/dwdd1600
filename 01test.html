Digital Citizenship

What is Digital Citizenship?

Digital citizenship is the responsible, safe, and ethical use of digital technology and the internet.

What is Digital Law?

Digital law refers to the rules and laws that govern how people use technology, computers, and the internet.

What is AUP?

An Acceptable Use Policy is a set of rules that explain how technology, networks, and internet services are allowed to be used in a school, workplace, or organization.

What is Plagiarism?

Plagiarism is when you use someone else's work, ideas, or words and present them as your own without giving credit.

What is Academic Dishonesty?

Academic dishonesty is any behavior that gives a student an unfair advantage or breaks the rules in school or learning activities.

It involves cheating, lying, or misrepresenting work instead of doing your own honest work.

What are the rules for AI Generated Content?

AI can help you learn, but it should not do the learning for you.
